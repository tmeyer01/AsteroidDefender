How to play:

Start the game by clicking on the Start Game button.

Asteroids, will start to fall from the sky.
Move the Defender and fire bullets at the asteriods to destroy them.

You have three lives. When an Asteroid hits the ground you will lose a life.
Watch out; once your score reaches 2500 points Asteroids will start to fall faster.

Controls:

To Move -
Use the "a" key to move left.
Use the "d" key to move right.
Click your mouse anywhere inside the canvas shoot.


Good luck. The fate of the earth is in your hands!
